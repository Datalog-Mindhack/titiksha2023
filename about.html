About Titiksha

TITIKSHA is a technical festival that has achieved great feats since its inception in 2008. The festival is held annually at Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University in Jammu and Kashmir, and it attracts a large crowd of young technocrats from all over India. TITIKSHA offers a variety of technical and non-technical events, entrepreneurial talks, and inspirational talks by eminent personalities. There will also be a game zone, adventure zone, musical concert, and an International Conference on Recent Trends and Advancements in Engineering and Technology. The festival expects a footfall of 5000 people only from J&K and attendees from around India are still counting. Team TITIKSHA is also planning to organize a road rally aimed at supporting the general cause of spreading information and making people aware of the dangers of drug abuse. Sponsorship opportunities are available, and the festival offers a great platform for advertising and brand promotion.
